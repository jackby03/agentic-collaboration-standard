Announcement: Repository relicensed to Apache-2.0

Short summary
We have relicensed the Agentic Collaboration Standard repository from MIT to Apache-2.0 to improve compatibility with foundation-level processes and institutional adoption.

Details
- Branch: `relicense/apache2`
- Pull request: https://github.com/jackby03/agentic-collaboration-standard/pull/new/relicense/apache2
- Files of interest: `LICENSE`, `NOTICE`, `docs/legal/licensing.md`, `MAINTAINERS.md`, `CONTRIBUTING.md`, `CHANGELOG.md`.

Why this change
Apache-2.0 includes a patent grant and is commonly accepted by foundations (Linux Foundation, AAIF). This relicensing reduces legal friction for institutional adoption and contributions.

What to expect
- No change to project functionality.
- Package manifests updated to reflect license change.
- We recommend reviewing `docs/legal/licensing.md` for details and next steps.

How to respond
- If you are a contributor and have concerns, please comment on the PR.
- For legal questions, contact the maintainer in `MAINTAINERS.md`.

Contact
